About the Application

Our Hospital Wait Time Predictor is an innovative AI-driven tool designed to estimate patient wait times in real-time. Built with Flask and powered by an XGBoost machine learning model, it analyzes key factors like hospital selection, urgency level, visit timing, nurse-to-patient ratio, and facility size to provide accurate predictions.

This helps hospital staff and patients plan better, reducing stress and improving overall efficiency. Whether you're a healthcare professional optimizing schedules or a patient seeking timely care, our tool delivers insights to make informed decisions.

How We Reduce Wait Times: Six Sigma and DMAIC Approach

We integrate Six Sigma principles—a data-driven methodology for eliminating defects and minimizing variability—with the DMAIC framework to provide actionable recommendations for reducing hospital wait times.

Define

Identify the problem: Long wait times affecting patient satisfaction and operational efficiency. Set goals for reduction targets.

Measure

Collect data on current wait times using our predictor tool, tracking metrics like average wait, peak hours, and influencing factors.

Analyze

Use AI insights to pinpoint root causes, such as staffing shortages or high-urgency influx during specific times.

Improve

Implement solutions like dynamic staffing adjustments, priority queuing, or process optimizations based on predictions.

Control

Monitor improvements with ongoing predictions and establish controls to sustain reduced wait times over time.

Six Sigma Integration

Aiming for near-perfect processes (3.4 defects per million opportunities), we apply statistical tools to minimize variability in wait times.
